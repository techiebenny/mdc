About MDC

The Microsoft Developer Club of RIT is a student-led community under the wing of Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors, which aims to provide technical and developmental skills inspiring everyone to pursue their goals and give them the push they need. We conduct events all over campus and beyond.

The domains we specialize in are :

  • Web Development
  • Cloud Computing
  • Hybrid Application Development
  • Python and its Applications
